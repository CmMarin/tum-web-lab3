About Kizomba

What is Kizomba?

Kizomba is an Angolan partner dance that emerged in the late 1980s. It blends traditional Angolan rhythms with Brazilian and Caribbean influences, creating a slow, sensual, and deeply musical dance form.

Vaidoso Style

We teach authentic kizomba with focus on proper technique, connection between partners, and musicality. Whether you're a beginner or experienced dancer, you'll find your place in our community.
